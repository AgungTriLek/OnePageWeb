Talking Tiktok

keeping up with every new platform, tool, and opportunity is a thankless gig.
But Tiktok isn't new and chances are its not going anywhere.

And you've probably been resisting diving in, telling yourself it's for "the youth"
even as we see adoption from political figures, brands, and entrepreneurs.
Here's a couple quick tips on getting started on Tiktok.

Step 1:

Download Tiktok (no that's not an affiliate link, its just how you get started)

Step 2:

Understand that TikTok is the #1 short videosocial media
platform IN THE WORLD and is crucial role in reaching more audiences

Here are some tips

  • Define your niche
  • Test out different content types and see what's working
  • Pick hashtags that match your audience and niche
  • Use user-generated content (it's free production!)
  • Features like stitch/duet can help SMEs interact with their users and create more content that hits
  • Don't be overly precious about your production this isn't a TVC or even an Instagram post. Let the guardrails down
  • TikTok’s algorithm will push your content as long as you keep posting

Make your workplace people-first

  • The pandemic accelerated the adoption of remote/hybrid working styles,
    opening the eyes of employees to what a true work-life balance could look like.
  • Now work-life balance is one of the most important factors to employees
  • But many companies haven't adapted their growth and
    performance plans to this reality
  • So start fixing it: dive into the needs of employees and open up
    communication channels
  • Ask yourself - when was the last time you asked your employees
    what they want from their workplace?
